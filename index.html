About

Hi am Sabarish Ramesh.I am an pencil sketching artist.I am learning pencil sketch for last 3 years.In this 3 years,I have faced lots of struggles as an artist.I practised alot and that gives me lots of patience and tricks.It takes long time to get lots of tricks but i share my experience and some of my tips and tricks to all of you guys!!

dont's

  • First don't shy or think about the output while drawing because it create lots of diversions ,just draw freely also don't hold the pencils tightly,if you hold tightly its hard to erase the art
  • Second don't feel I don't have enough materials,draw with whatever you have
  • Third if you have mind diversions or not interested to draw ,then never touch the art in that moment,because it can't give your 100% output
SABARISH RAMESH

TIPS

  • use apsara 2h,2b,8b pencils of drawing
  • use brushes and pencil stumps for smooth blending
  • use mono zero eraser,electric eraser for detailed skintones
  • initially drawing takes lots of time but don't get stress and discourage you
  • take me as an example,even I became an artist why you can't
